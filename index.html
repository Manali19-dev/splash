Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-learning?

E-learning is a method of learning that utilizes electronic technologies to access educational curriculum outside of a traditional classroom setting. It allows learners to study from anywhere at any time.

How do I sign up for courses?

You can sign up for courses by visiting our "Courses" section and selecting the course you want to enroll in. Click on the "Enroll Now" button to register.

Do I need any special software to access the courses?

No special software is required. Our courses are accessible through any modern web browser. However, we recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

Can I access the courses on my mobile device?

Yes, our e-learning platform is fully responsive and can be accessed on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. You can learn on-the-go with ease.

What if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical issues, please contact our support team via the "Contact Us" section on our website. We are here to help you with any problems you might face.
